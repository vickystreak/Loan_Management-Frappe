## Loan Tracking and Managing App

#### The Frappe-based loan management system simplifies loan and borrower management through five key document types: Borrower, Loan Application, Loan (created upon approval), Payment, and Payment Receipt. These documents collectively provide a structured approach to borrower information, application processing, loan tracking, repayment management, and receipt generation.


### Borrower: Stores borrower information, contact details, and social security/Aadhar number serves for risk assessment.
![Screenshot from 2023-10-19 15-57-34](https://github.com/vickystreak/Loan_Management-Frappe/assets/82258418/7176bc44-9cd7-44f9-b9f9-2d9e421a1e48)

### Loan Application: Captures loan application details, including borrower, loan type, amount, interest rates, and status (approved, pending, rejected),Assists in loan evaluation and EMI calculation.
![Screenshot from 2023-10-19 15-57-20](https://github.com/vickystreak/Loan_Management-Frappe/assets/82258418/acabd729-1f31-4e40-a1a0-644a44612bdc)

### Loan (Created upon Approval): Records comprehensive loan information, including borrower, loan type, EMI details, total loan amount, and outstanding balance.
![Screenshot from 2023-10-19 15-56-56](https://github.com/vickystreak/Loan_Management-Frappe/assets/82258418/252126fc-961e-4437-98b2-fefeb8a86f7e)

### Payment: Manages repayment records, including payment amount, date, and associated borrower,Updates the outstanding loan amount and EMI payment count,Generates receipts for each payment.
![Screenshot from 2023-10-19 15-56-18](https://github.com/vickystreak/Loan_Management-Frappe/assets/82258418/905bac47-f35e-40cd-a321-9957dda60db8)

### Payment Receipt: Captures payment receipt details, providing proof of payment,Offers printable receipts with loan status updates,Maintains a transaction history for reference.
